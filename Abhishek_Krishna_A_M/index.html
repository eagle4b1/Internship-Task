About HackSphere

HackSphere 2025 is the ultimate global hackathon experience, bringing together the brightest minds from around the world for 48 hours of non-stop innovation. Whether you're a seasoned developer, creative designer, or passionate creator, this is your chance to push boundaries and build something extraordinary.

Join thousands of participants from over 50 countries as we tackle real-world challenges, forge new connections, and compete for amazing prizes. With mentorship from industry leaders, cutting-edge workshops, and unlimited creative freedom, HackSphere is where your next big idea comes to life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HackSphere 2025?
HackSphere 2025 is a 48-hour global online hackathon where developers, designers, and creators collaborate to build innovative solutions to real-world problems.
Who can participate?
Anyone with a passion for technology and innovation! Whether you're a student, professional, or hobbyist, all skill levels are welcome.
Is there a registration fee?
No! HackSphere 2025 is completely free to participate. We believe in making innovation accessible to everyone.
What prizes are available?
We have over $50,000 in prizes including cash awards, tech gadgets, mentorship opportunities, and exclusive internship offers from our partner companies.
Do I need a team?
Teams are recommended but not required! You can participate solo or form teams of up to 4 members. We'll have team formation sessions at the beginning.
